Lemon Blueberry Cheesecake Parfaits

Description

These parfaits are a simple and elegant dessert. The creamy cheesecake layer is paired with a zesty lemon flavor and topped with a sweet blueberry compote. The layers are built in individual cups, making them perfect for serving guests or as a personal treat!

Ingredients
    For cheesecake layer:
  • 8 oz cream cheese (softened)
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (chilled)

    • For the Blueberry Compote:
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp lemon zest

    • For the Crust:
  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp melted butter

Method
  1. Prepare the Crust: In a small bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Mix well until the crumbs are fully coated in butter. Spoon a small amount into the bottom of each serving cup or jar to form the crust layer.
  2. Make the Blueberry Compote: In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine blueberries, sugar, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Cook, stirring occasionally, for about 5-7 minutes, or until blueberries burst and the mixture is slightly thickened. Remove from heat and let cool.
  3. Make the Cheesecake Layer: Beat cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract in a big mixing bowl until creamy and smooth. Whip heavy cream in another bowl until stiff peaks form. Gently fold whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture until combined.
  4. Assemble the Parfaits: In each serving cup, make a layer of graham cracker crust, topped with a layer of cheesecake mixture, and spoon over a generous helping of blueberry compote. Repeat the layers until the cup is full.
  5. Chill: Chill the parfaits in the refrigerator for a minimum of 2 hours, or until chilled and set.
  6. Serve: Garnish with extra blueberries or a sprinkle of lemon zest for color and flavor.

Why To Try!

These parfaits are the perfect marriage of creamy, tangy, and sweet. The refreshing blueberry compote and airy cheesecake filling result in a divine dessert without feeling too rich.
