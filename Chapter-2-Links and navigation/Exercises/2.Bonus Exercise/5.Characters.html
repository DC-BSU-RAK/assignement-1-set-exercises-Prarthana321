Characters

    This page consists of the characters of ever after high.

  1. Apple White:
    • Daughter of Snow White.
    • Apple White is known for her leadership qualities and commitment to her royal destiny.

  2. Briar Beauty:
    • Daughter of Sleeping Beauty.
    • Briar Beauty is characterized by her laid-back attitude and love for adventure.

  3. Raven Queen:
    • Daughter of the Evil Queen.
    • Raven Queen is the one who grapples with her legacy and desires to forge her own path.

  4. Lizzie Hearts:
    • Daughter of the Queen of Hearts.
    • Lizzie Heart navigates the expectations of her royal lineage.

  5. Kitty Cheshire:
    • Daughter of the Cheshire Cat.
    • Kitty Cheshire is known for her mischievous nature and ability to disappear.

  6. Madeline Hatter:
    • Daughter of the Mad Hatter.
    • Madeline Hatter has a quirky personality and a love for tea parties.

  7. Alistair Wonderland:
    • Son of Alice.
    • Alistair Wonderland is the one who brings a sense of wonder and curiosity to the group.

  8. Bunny Blanc:
    • Daughter of the White Rabbit.
    • Bunny Blanc always punctual and a bit of a perfectionist.

  9. Courtly Jester:
    • A new character introduced as the vice-principal of Wonderland High
    • Courtly Jester is the one who has her own agenda.
